About Me

From Culinary Arts to Computer Science

Professional Background

I began my career in the culinary arts, obtaining my certification from Fitjar Vidaregåande Skule. This culinary foundation laid the groundwork for my detail-oriented approach and ability to perform effectively in high-pressure environments.

My career evolved as I moved into industrial roles, working at Nova Sea, Bremnes Seashore, and as a cleaner and fire watch at Subshore AS on the Njord project. These positions have honed my skills in safety protocols, precision work, and collaboration.

Contact: bjornmagnekristensen1999jobb@gmail.com

Technical Journey

While building my industrial career, I've developed a growing interest in technology and programming. This led me to Harvard's CS50 course, where I've been learning computer science fundamentals and building various projects.

I've found many parallels between my industrial work and programming – both require attention to detail, methodical problem-solving, and an ability to adapt to changing requirements.

My technical portfolio includes various small applications and tools that demonstrate my growing programming skills. These projects range from practical utilities to interactive web applications.
