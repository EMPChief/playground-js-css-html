About Me

From Culinary Arts to Computer Science

My Background

My journey began in the culinary arts, where I honed my skills as a chef at Fitjar VGS. The precision, creativity, and problem-solving required in the kitchen have surprisingly paralleled my transition into programming. Just as each dish requires careful planning and execution, each line of code contributes to creating something meaningful.

The decision to transition into software development came from my growing fascination with technology and its potential to create solutions that impact people's lives. This led me to Harvard's CS50, where I've discovered my passion for programming.

CS50 Journey

CS50 has been a transformative experience, taking me from writing my first line of code to building complex applications. Through challenges like implementing data structures in C, creating web applications with Flask, and working with databases, I've developed a strong foundation in computer science principles.

Each problem set has been a stepping stone, teaching me not just programming languages but also how to think algorithmically and solve problems efficiently. The course has equipped me with both technical skills and the confidence to tackle complex challenges.
